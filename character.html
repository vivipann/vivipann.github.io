Yumi Akuma

A young Japanese schoolgirl with a deceptive innocence veiling a deep-seated darkness.

Cunning and manipulative.

Driven by her desire for power and an insatiable thirst for revenge.

Beneath her hardened exterior lies a fractured soul, haunted by the horrors of a world gone mad.

Takeshi Nakamura

Reluctant hero thrust into a world torn apart by chaos and despair.

Armed with courage and determination,his every action is driven by a steadfast commitment to protect those he holds dear.

Haunted by the ghosts of his past, he finds strength in his unwavering sense of duty.



Researcher

Beacon of hope amidst the darkness, tirelessly working to unravel the mysteries of the mutagenic virus that has ravaged humanity.

Kaito Sato

A mysterious figure shrouded in darkness, his true intentions masked behind a facade of cold calculation.

Immune to the mutagenic virus.

Highly intellectual.

Beneath his enigmatic exterior lies a deep-seated rage, driving him to seek domination over a world that has descended into madness.

The Old Factory

A silent sentinel amidst the ruins, its towering silhouette a testament to humanity's ingenuity and industry.

Once a hub of productivity and innovation, it now lies dormant, its machinery idle and its halls deserted.

The factory retains an air of eerie majesty, its rusted walls and broken windows bearing witness to the passage of time.



Yumi's hideout

A clandestine refuge nestled within the heart of the ruined cityscape, concealed from prying eyes by the veil of darkness.

Exudes an aura of danger and intrigue.

Inside, the air is heavy with the scent of decay, mingling with the faint echoes of Yumi's sinister whispers.

The City

Once a bustling metropolis teeming with life and vitality, now lies in ruins, its towering skyscrapers reduced to crumbling edifices that loom over the desolate landscape.

Danger lurks around every corner, with mutated creatures and marauding bands of survivors haunting the shadows.
